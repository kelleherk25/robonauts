Everybot

FRC 118, the Robonauts, are a hall of fame team based out of Houston, Texas. They are known for their close relation to NASA, their signature gold and white color scheme, their mold-breaking robot designs, and their community work that has earned them many impact awards. Their most famous project is the Everybot, a simple, robust, and affordable robot they design every year for newer or less experienced teams to start off with. Below are all the Everybots:

2025

Reefscape
Picks up coral from the station, scores on L1, picks up algae from the ground, scores in the processor, and climbs on the deep cage.

2024

Crescendo
<>Picks up notes from the source, scores in the speaker, scores in the amp, and climbs on the chain.

2023

Charged Up
Picks up cones and cubes from the double substation, scores on L1, L2, and L3, and balances on the charging station.

2022

Rapid React
Picks up cargo from the ground, scores in the low hub, climbs on the low bar, and climbs on the mid bar.

2020

Infinite Recharge
Picks up power cells from the ground, holds up to 5 power cells scores in the low goal, and climbs on the hangar.

2019

Deep Space
Picks up cargo from the ground and the loading station, picks up hatch panels from the loading station, climbs L1 of the habitat.

2018

Power Up
Picks up cubes from the loading station, scores in the low goal, and plays defense because of its tank drive.
